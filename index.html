About ArtisanMarket

Our Story

ArtisanMarket was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to create a thriving marketplace where artisans and crafters could connect directly with customers who appreciate handmade, unique products.

We believe that behind every handmade item is a story, a passion, and a person who deserves to be supported for their craft.

Our Values

  • Supporting Artisans: We're committed to helping artisans grow their businesses.
  • Quality over Quantity: We carefully curate our marketplace to ensure high-quality products.
  • Community Building: We foster connections between makers and buyers.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a seller account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button and select "Become a Seller." Follow the prompts to set up your account.

What are the fees for selling?

We charge a 5% transaction fee on each sale. There are no listing fees or monthly subscriptions.

How do payments work?

Buyers pay through our secure platform. Funds are released to sellers after order completion.

What should I do if I have an issue with an order?

Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery, and we'll help resolve the issue.

How long does it take for my listing to appear?

Listings are typically reviewed within 24 hours and published if they meet our guidelines.

Do you offer seller protection?

Yes, we offer protection against fraudulent chargebacks and unauthorized transactions.
